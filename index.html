HABEWALI DAL TADKA (SPICED LENTILS – DHABA STYLE)

×

Ingredients:

2 tbsp light cooking oil
1 tsp cumin seeds
1 tsp fennel seeds
3-4 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
1-2 green chillies, finely chopped
1 large onion, finely chopped
1/2 tsp red chili powder
1/4 tsp turmeric
1 tsp coriander powder
1 medium-sized tomato, finely chopped
1-inch piece of ginger, julienned
salt, to taste
2 cups split pea lentils, boiled in a pressure cooker till soft but still making sure each grain still holds its shape
water, as needed
fresh coriander leaves, chopped for garnish
squeeze of lemon, for garnish

Directions:

Heat oil in a large heavy bottomed pan and sauté cumin seeds, fennel seeds, garlic, green chillies and onions till lightly browned and fragrant. Add in spices, and fry for a few seconds.

Mix in the chopped tomatoes, ginger and salt, and cook for a few minutes to combine well. Add in lentils, and water depending on the consistency you like, and let it simmer on low heat covered for 4-5 minutes. This will allow all the flavours to blend in well.

Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and squeeze of lemon juice, and serve warm.

Mug Cake Recipe No Egg

×

Ingredients:

  • All-purpose flour 4 tbsp
  • White sugar, 2 tbsp
  • Baking powder, ½ tsp
  • Milk 4 tbsp 
  • Vanilla extract ¼ tsp
  • Oil 1 ½ tbsp
  • Funfetti 1 tbsp for sprinkling at the top

The cupcake is the cake prepared in a cup. The best thing about this cake, it is easy to prepare but have a really yummy taste. We are preparing here vanilla cake in a cup; you can try any other versions like chocolate cake, strawberry cake, etc. So, next time when guests come to your home offer them cake in a mug.

DIRECTIONS

  1. For this recipe, you can use microwave friendly mugs. Otherwise, the cake will not cook properly.
  2. Now take a bowl. Add all purpose flour in it, put baking powder in the cake and mix both ingredients well.
  3. Add small oil of your choice, preferably oil that does not have any flavor. You can also use soft butter, sugar and mix all ingredients well. Pour milk in it to prepare a batter with required consistency. Now, add vanilla extract in the mix and blend it with the help of a blender.
  4. Sprinkle funfetti on the top. The cake batter is not ready to serve. Transfer this mix in the cup in which you want to bake this cake, and put it in the microwave for 1 minute or 1 minute 10 seconds. The cake in cup takes less time to bake, therefore, adjusts the temperature accordingly.
  5. Touch cake’s top to check if it has been cooked and take it out. Serve it hot with ice cream or frosting of your choice.

TIPS

  • Check the consistency of cake before baking to get a soft delicious cake.
  • Top it with tutti fruity.

Lemon Orzo Salad

×

Ingredients:

  • Lemon (1)
  • Olive oil-extra virgin 20-30 ml
  • Raw Orzo Pasta 1 cup
  • Basil leaves fresh 1 full cup
  • Tomatoes medium size (2)
  • Pepper and salt according to the taste

This is a simple Orzo Pasta Salad. This salad is super easy to prepare and a perfect one for summer picnics. This is a great recipe for barbecues and potlucks as well! This is a side dish that everyone can enjoy with their main course meal. 

DIRECTIONS

  1. From lemon you have to get the lemon zest. The zest you can remove with the help of a small grater or you can also use microplane. Keep the zest in a large bowl and keep it aside.
  2. Now, cut the zested lemon in half and squeeze lemon juice in the same bowl you have taken the zest. At this step, you have to make sure that no seeds are coming into the mix. In this juice, add olive oil, pepper, and salt. Mix all the ingredients with a light hand and keep it aside.
  3. Cook the Orzo Pasta with lots of salt. Cook it till it gets a soft texture. Take out a half cup of pasta water and keep it aside. Drain out excess water from pasta and add the cooked pasta in lemon zest mix which we prepare in the bowl.
  4. Mix all ingredients well and add half-cup pasta water in the bowl. The dish will look little water but don’t worry; the paste will absorb the excess water.
  5. Allow Orzo to cool at the room temperature. Once it cools down, add chopped tomato, chopped basil leaves and mix everything well. Take out the Orzo in an air-tight container and keep it in the refrigerator for two hours. After two hours, the dish becomes ready to serve.

TIPS

  • You can add seasoning in dish according to your taste.
  • If you don’t have fresh lemon, then you can add lemon juice.
